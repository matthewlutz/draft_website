## Sonny Styles

Ohio State, 6'5 243, 21 Y/O

### Pros 

- 4.48 40 
- Violent hands 
- Natural leverage 
- Played Safety his first 2 years 
- Stacks and sheds blocks with ease 
- Great football instincts 
- ELITE cover skills in zone
- Amazing length and frame for LB 
- Great play recognition 
- Arrives with violence
- Sideline to sideline range 
- Can affect passing lanes with his long arms 
- Ascending prospect, 14 missed tackles in 2024, 2 in 2025 (PFF)
### Cons
- Struggles in man coverage
- Would like to see improved pad level
- Only a 2 year starter at Linebacker
- Overaggressive on play action and RPO's

## Arvell Reese
Ohio State, 6'4, 243, 20 Y/O

### Pros
- 20 Y/O
- 4.5 speed
- Exceptional size and length
- Great stacking/shedding in the run game
- VERY sounds tackler
- Great lateral speed
- Strength at the point of attack
- Good first step when playing on the edge
- Excellent playing spy role
- Meets pulling blockers and attacks them
- Fantastic when blitzing from LB
- Power rush is very promising, very small sample size
- Very versatile, Can play at iLB, EDGE, and even has some snaps at slot

### Cons
- Still very raw, especially on the edge
- Struggles in coverage
- Bites hard on play-action
- Very limited experience at both LB and EDGE
- Over aggressiveness filling gaps sometimes forgoing his contain responsibility
- Was very quiet end of 2025 (15 tackles in final 6 games vs 41 tackles in first 6 games)
- Got washed playing on the edge a lot the second half of the year

## CJ Allen
Georgia, 6'1 235, 20 Y/O

### Pros
- 8 TFL's in 2025
- Green dot at Georgia
- Exceptional burst and play speed
- EXTREMELY reliable tackler. 6% missed tackle rate over the last two years
- Makes people feel him when tackling
- Great speed
- Incredible lateral quickness
- High level run defender
- Flies downhill with decisiveness 
- Good anchor against pulling blockers

### Cons
- Mediocre size
- Arm length
- Not very many splash plays throughout his career (INT's, FF's, sacks)
- Struggles in coverage (TBF his coverage assignments were very tough at UG)
- Bites easily on play action plays
- Trouble stacking and shedding blockers
