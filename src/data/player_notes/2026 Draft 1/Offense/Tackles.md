## Francis Mauigoa
 6'6 325 20 Y/O

### Pros
- 20 Y/O
- Absolute BEAST in run-blocking
- Great strike timing and hand technique
- Freak athlete (Feldmans freak list)
- Great movement skills, hits the second level with quickness
- Violent hands
- Great strength when anchoring
- Has significantly improved year after year
- Great anchoring strength
- Low center of gravity

### Cons
- Against complex blitz packages, his processing speed is very much a work in progress
- Short arms for a tackle (Have not been able to find a verified number, but most reports are they're short)
- When he gets beat it's often to the inside
- Miami runs almost no zone runs
- Lunges forward in pass protection occasionally
- Would like to see blocks get finished more consistently 

## Spencer Fano
Utah, 6'6 308, 21 Y/O

### Pros
- Gave up 0 sacks and only 5 hurries in 2025
- Only gave up 1 sack in 2024
- Fantastic in the run. 
- Played LT his freshman year
- 3 years of production
- Great movement skills
- Quick feet
- Slides effortlessly with pass rushers
- Gets up to the second level with ease
- Elite recovery skills

### Cons
- Very weak anchor against bull rushes
- Could add more mass to his frame
- 5 penalties in 2025
- Sub 33 inch arms
- His initial strike doesn't generate very much knockback
